About Liberty Discussion Forum

Liberty Discussion Forum is an online platform developed by the students of Liberty College to promote academic and technical discussions among peers and faculty. It serves as a collaborative space where users can ask questions, share knowledge, and engage in meaningful conversations related to their studies, technology trends, programming, and more. The forum encourages interactive learning by allowing students to post queries, respond to others, and even share image-based questions through a user-friendly interface. With features like secure login, user roles, and dedicated sections for discussions and user reviews, the platform aims to build a strong academic community. Designed using Django for the backend and styled with Tailwind CSS and custom styles, the project follows the Agile development approach and provides students with practical experience in building a full-fledged web application.
